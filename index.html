Core Caseworks LLC started as my own personal need for an attractive wall mount computer case to house my media center/gaming machine. It needed to accomodate full ATX cards and have gaming quality air flow. It also needed to pass the wife test for aesthetics in the living room.

I started a mod blog at bit-tech.net where it was voted mod of the month and then nominated for mod of the year. I made a couple dozen for sale and the Core Caseworks computer case can be found in conference and media rooms across the US.

Techforce Pro is a website for low voltage integrators to easily create wiring diagrams, and manage their customers based on what they installed, and where they installed them.

This project has evolved from being a simple wiring diagram creation tool to being a full customer management tool with a companion mobile app.
